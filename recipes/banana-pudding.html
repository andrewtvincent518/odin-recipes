The Best Dang Ole Banana Pudding

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cold milk
  • 1 (5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 1 (12 ounce) container frozen whipped topping, thawed (or not
  • 1 (16 ounce) package vanilla wafers
  • 14 bananas, sliced

Steps

  1. Place milk and pudding mix in a large bowl; beat the devil outta it for 2 minutes. Blend in condensed milk until smooth. Stir in vanilla, then fold in whipped topping. (If you don't know what folding is, go to this link: I'm a silly goose
  2. Layer wafers, banana slices, and pudding mixture in a glass serving bowl. Chill until serving. Both you and the pudding bro!
